Lentil Bolognese

Description:

A tasty vegan bolognese made with lentils, seasonal vegetables, and pasta.

Credit: Gittemary Johansen

Ingredients

  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • Â½ dl of olive oil
  • 2 dl of red lentils (dry)
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 dl of seasonal mushrooms
  • Fresh basil
  • Rosemary
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Chop the onion and the garlic.
  2. Fry it on the bottom of a cast-iron skillet in hot olive oil for 3 minutes.
  3. When the onions are starting to brown, add finely diced mushrooms, stir for 1 minute and then add the content of one can diced tomatoes.
  4. Fill the empty can with water and add that as well.
  5. Let it simmer for a few minutes, then add the lentils along with another can's worth of water.
  6. Close the lid and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Stir every minute to make sure nothing sticks to the bottom.
  8. Add your spice mix of choice, I prefer a good combo of fresh basil, rosemary, and oregano, along with salt and pepper.
  9. Let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes or until the sauce is thick.
  10. Serve with rice, potatoes or pasta.
